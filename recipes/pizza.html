Pizza recipe

To make a delicious homemade pizza, start by preheating your oven to 475°F (245°C). For the dough, combine 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast, 1 tsp sugar, and ½ tsp salt in a bowl. Mix in ¾ cup warm water and 2 tbsp olive oil, knead for about 5 minutes, and let it rise for 1 hour. For the sauce, blend 1 cup tomato sauce with 1 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp garlic powder, ½ tsp dried basil, and a pinch of salt. Roll out the dough on a floured surface, spread the sauce evenly over it, then top with 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings (like pepperoni, bell peppers, and onions). Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • yeast
  • salt
  • sugar
  • water
  • oil
  • tomato sauce
  • dried oregano
  • garlic powder
  • dried basil
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • any extra toppings you like

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix in warm water and olive oil, then knead the dough for about 5 minutes. Let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. For the sauce, mix tomato sauce with oregano, garlic powder, basil, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface, spread the sauce evenly over it, and top with shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Slice and enjoy!
